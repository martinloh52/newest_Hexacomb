Hex Rules

  1. Players take turns placing in one counter on an empty hexagonal cell of the board
  2. Once a counter is placed, it belongs to that color, and canâ€™t be used or changed by the other player
  3. Counter can be placed anywhere on the board as long as it is on an empty hexagonal cell
  4. The goal is to make a continuous path from two sides of player's corresponding colour
  5. Player who finishes their path first, wins
